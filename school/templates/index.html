About Us

Itech academy is a leading career-directing institute that supports students in their journey of reaching their ultimate life goal. We have a team of expert career counsellors, who help the students in fulfilling their dreams. Apart from including aptitude testing, educational counselling, and career guidance, we also provide psychometric sessions to realise the inner talent of each student.

Our team also helps the students to know about the possibilities and scope of various courses, thus supporting them to unknot the confusions in their career.A team of career counsellors will help the students to realise the strengths, interests, and abilities of students.
